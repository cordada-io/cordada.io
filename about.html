About

DESCRIPTION

We are Cordada, a Spanish software company dedicated to developing innovative AI applications.

As a remote-first organization, we leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence to create transformative technological solutions.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of AI technology, delivering intelligent tools that help our clients thrive in an increasingly digital world.

NAME

Cordada is the Catalan word for a group of people tied together by a rope while climbing or doing any mountain activity.

We chose this name to represent our team spirit and our commitment and confidence to working together to achieve our goals.
